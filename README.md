A computational fluid dynamics study of a naval nozzle analysing internal 
flow behaviour, pressure distribution, and discharge efficiency using 
ANSYS Fluent for simulation and SolidWorks for geometry modelling.

---

## Overview

Naval nozzles control the directed flow of fluid to generate thrust or 
manage propulsion efficiency. This study investigates the internal flow 
field to evaluate velocity distribution, pressure drop, and nozzle 
performance under steady-state conditions.
